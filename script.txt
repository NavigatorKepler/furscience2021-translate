FurScience Summer 2021 Furry Fandom Study	FurScience2021夏季兽圈调研

Preamble	前言
If you are a furry and 18 years or older, you are invited to participate in this survey study about furries and the furry fandom. Please read the following information concerning the study to be conducted.	如果你是一名年满18岁的Furry/兽迷, 我们邀请你来参加这项关于兽迷与兽圈的研究. 请先阅读以下关于该研究的内容. 

Purpose of the study	研究目的
This survey is part of ongoing exploratory research by FurScience looking at the psychology of furries and the furry fandom. We will be collecting information about numerous topics with the hope of gaining a better understanding of the furry fandom. Specifically, we are exploring questions about furry and fursona identity, gender, relationships, race, sexual orientation, conflict, generational differences, authenticity, bullying, the functions of fandom, and leisure activities.	这项研究是FurScience对于兽迷与兽圈的持续探索研究的一部分. 我们将会收集许多方面的信息, 希望能够对我们的兽圈有更好的了解. 具体来说, 我们想了解关于兽迷与兽设的关系及身份认同、性别认同、情感关系、种族、性取向、冲突、代沟、真实性、霸凌、兽圈的影响以及休闲活动之类的问题.

Voluntary participation and right to withdraw	自愿参与和退出研究的权利
Participation in this research project is completely voluntary. You do not have to respond to any questions that make you feel uncomfortable and you have the right to withdraw from this study at any time with no consequences. If you decide to withdraw from the survey or want your data to be deleted and to not be analyzed by the researchers, you are asked to skip to the end of the survey and state in the final comments that you wish for your data to be deleted. You will still receive the confirmation code and be permitted to enter the draw if you choose to do this. You may also withdraw from the study by closing the browser window at any time – partial data will be deleted by the researchers. Please be advised, however, that if you close the browser window before getting to the end of the study, you will not receive the confirmation code.	该项研究的参与是完全自愿的。你不需要回答任何你不想回答的问题，并且你有权随时退出这项研究并不会有任何后果。如果你希望退出该项研究, 或希望从数据库中删除你的信息使其不再作为研究的一部分，请跳过所有问题直到问卷最后的留言区, 声明你希望删除这些数据。这样做你仍会获得验证码并有机会参与抽奖。你也可以选择通过中途关闭浏览器的方式来退出研究——研究员会手动删除不完整的问卷。然而，请注意，如果你在问卷结束之前关闭了浏览器，您将无法获得抽奖用的验证码。

SECURITY & ANONYMITY	匿名和隐私协议
Several steps will be taken to ensure the security and anonymity of survey data submitted. You will be completing the study by an online survey operated by Qualtrics. Qualtrics has implemented technical, administrative, and physical safeguards to protect the information provided via the Services from loss, misuse, and unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no Internet transmission is ever fully secure or error free. 	为了确保数据的安全性和匿名性，我们采取了一些措施。您将会在Qualtrics运营的平台上完成此次调查。Qualtrics已经采用了技术、管理和物理保护措施，以保护通过服务提供的信息免遭丢失、误用、盗窃、披露、更改或破坏。但是，没有绝对安全且零失误的网络连接。
When information is transmitted over the Internet, privacy cannot be guaranteed. There is always a risk your responses may be intercepted by a third party (e.g., government agencies, hackers). The researchers will not collect or use Internet protocol (IP) addresses or other information which could link your participation to your computer or electronic device.	我们不能保证信息在传输过程中的隐私性。有可能您的信息在传输过程中会被第三方截获（比如防火墙和骇客）。但是研究员不会收集IP地址等信息，将您的设备与此次问卷相关联。

There will not be any printed material or printing of any raw data from this study.	这些数据将由以下研究员使用(Dr. Courtney Plante – Bishop’s University; Dr. Stephen Reysen, Texas A&M University – Commerce, Dr. Kathleen Gerbasi, Niagara County Community College; Dr. Sharon Roberts, Renison University College – University of Waterloo)，以及他们的助手和研究合作方。数据将保存在研究员们有密码保护的终端设备上，并保留五年，五年后将被彻底删除。收集到的原始数据不会被打印到纸质文件上。
